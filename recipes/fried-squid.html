Hotpot

Chinese Hot Pot is an interactive meal in which diners sit around a simmering pot of soup at the center of the table with various raw ingredients—meat, seafood, vegetables, tofu, and starches—in thin slices or small pieces for quick cooking.

✔️食材： 鱿鱼须、葱姜蒜、洋葱、青线椒、小米辣 · ✔️调味： 豆瓣酱、料酒、味极鲜、蚝油、老抽、白糖、烧烤料 · ㊙️㊙️做法： 1️⃣、鱿鱼须+葱段+姜片+料酒，冷水下锅，焯水3分钟后捞出，清水洗净。 2️⃣、调酱汁：1勺味极鲜+1勺蚝油+半勺老抽+小半勺白糖。 3️⃣、锅中倒油，下姜片+蒜末+豆瓣酱炒出红油。 4️⃣、倒入鱿鱼须和酱汁，再加入青红椒+洋葱翻炒均匀，出锅前再加入1勺烧烤料即可。

Ingredients

  • sliced beef sirloin
  • sliced lamb

Steps

  1. Prepare the broth.
  2. Prepare the dipping sauces.
  3. Prepare the ingredients.
  4. Enjoy!
